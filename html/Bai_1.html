Bài 1. Phương pháp sai phân hữu hạn¶

Gi s chng ta phi tm nghim phng trnh o hm ring (PTHR):

$$\frac {\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} = 0\ trn\ min\ 0 \leq x \leq 1, 0 \leq y \leq 1$$

.

S dng phng php tnh khng cho kt qu nghim chnh xc u(x,y) lin tc (u ti mi im) trn ton b vng tnh ton m ch cho nghim gn ng ti cc im ri rc. Do trc ht chng ta cn ri rc ha min tnh ton. Gi s, chia on $0 \leq x \leq 1$ thnh (nx-1) on bng nhau c di $\Delta x$, chia on $0 \leq y \leq 1$ thnh (ny-1) on bng nhau c di $\Delta y$, ta thu c li ri rc c $(nx-1)(ny-1)$ li v $(nx)(ny)$ im li. Khi nghim chnh xc u(x, y) s c thay th bng nghim gn ng $u(i\Delta x, j\Delta y)$ ti cc im li (i, j), vi i l ch s theo trc x, j l ch s theo trc y. vit gn, ta quy c: $u(i\Delta x, j\Delta) y = u_{i, j}$. Vi $i\Delta x = x_0, j\Delta y = y_0$ ta c:

\begin{array}{c} u(x_0, y_0) = u_{i, j}\\ u(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0) = u_{i+1, j}\\ u(x_0 - \Delta x, y_0) = u_{i-1, j}\\ u(x_0, y_0 + \Delta y) = u_{i, j+1}\\ u(x_0, y_0 - \Delta y) = u_{i, j-1}\\ \end{array}

Trung hp y l thi gian ta dng ch s 'n' v vit thnh $u_i^n$. Tip theo, chng ta s dng li ri rc nh th no?

Trc khi tr li cu hi ny ta s thc hnh mt bi tp nh: s dng python chia li, v li. y ta s dng hai th vin: numpy tnh ton mng, ma trn; pyplot - v th. chia li n gin, ta s dng hm linspace trong numpy.

Quay li vi li ri rc. Theo nh ngha o hm ring: $$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(x_0, y_0) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac {u(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0) - u(x_0, y_0)}{\Delta x}$$ Nh vy nu nh $\Delta x$ nh th ta c xp x: $$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(x_0, y_0) \approx \frac{u(x_0+\Delta x, y_0)-u(x_0, y_0)}{\Delta x}$$ chnh xc ca php xp x sai phn ny c th xem xt thng qua khai trin Taylor. Ta c: $$u(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0) = u(x_0, y_0) + \Delta x \frac {\partial u}{\partial x}(x_0, y_0) + \frac {(\Delta x)^2}{2!} \frac {\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}(x_0, y_0) + \frac {(\Delta x)^3}{3!} \frac {\partial^3 u}{\partial x^3}(x_0, y_0) + ... \ (1) $$ Bin i phng trnh trn ta thu c s sai phn: $$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}(x_0, y_0) = \frac{u(x_0+\Delta x, y_0)-u(x_0, y_0)}{\Delta x} + O(\Delta x)$$ Hay ta c: $$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{u_{i+1, j}-u_{i, j}}{\Delta x} + O(\Delta x)$$ S ny cn c gi l s sai phn phi (v 'bc sang phi i->i+1').

y, $O(\Delta x)$ gi l sai s php xp x, n c gi tr tuyt i khng vt qu $k|\Delta x|, khi\ \Delta x \to 0, k>0$.

Trong trng hp ny ta ni php xp x c chnh xc bc mt, tng ng s bc 1. Nu php xp x c sai s dng $O(\Delta x^2)$, ta ni php xp x c chnh xc bc hai, tng ng s bc 2. Nh vy nu $\Delta x$ nh th s bc 2 chnh xc hn s bc 1. Tuy nhin gi tr $\Delta x$ chng no l " nh", "nh va " th cn phi xem xt.

Ngoi s sai phn phi, chng ta c th xy dng v s cc s khc nhau cho o hm ring $\partial u / \partial x$ vi bc khc nhau. V d:

  • s sai phn tri bc 1: Khai trin Taylor: $$ u(x_0 - \Delta x, y_0) = u(x_0, y_0) - \Delta x \frac {\partial u}{\partial x}(x_0, y_0) + \frac {(\Delta x)^2}{2!} \frac {\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}(x_0, y_0) - \frac {(\Delta x)^3}{3!} \frac {\partial^3 u}{\partial x^3}(x_0, y_0) + ... \ (2) $$ ta c: $$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{u_{i, j}-u_{i-1, j}}{\Delta x} + O(\Delta x)$$
  • s sai phn trung tm bc 2: Tr hai v phng trnh (1) v (2) ta c: $$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{u_{i+1, j}-u_{i-1, j}}{2\Delta x} + O(\Delta x)^2$$

Đạo hàm bậc hai:¶

Cng hai v phng trnh (1) v (2) ta c mt dng s sai phn cho o hm bc hai:

$$\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} = \frac{u_{i+1, j}-2u_{i, j}+u_{i-1, j}}{(\Delta x)^2} + O(\Delta x)^2$$

Vy, s dng s sai phn nh th no gii PTHR? Cu hi ny s c lm r [bi 2].

Trc khi kt thc bi 1 v s sai phn, chng ta hy th lng mt cht :)
